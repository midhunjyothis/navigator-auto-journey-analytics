# KPI Definitions (v0)

## North Star Metric

### Purchase Conversion Rate
Definition:
- Number of journeys reaching Purchase
- Divided by number of journeys entering Browse

Grain:
- Customer–vehicle journey

Primary Use:
- Measure overall effectiveness of the Navigator experience.

---

## Funnel KPIs

### Browse → Eligibility Conversion Rate
Definition:
- Journeys with Eligibility stage
- Divided by journeys with Browse stage

Primary Use:
- Identify eligibility friction by segment, vehicle price, and channel.

### Eligibility → Lead Conversion Rate
Definition:
- Journeys with Lead stage
- Divided by journeys with Eligibility stage

Primary Use:
- Measure quality of offers and mid-funnel persuasion.

### Lead → Purchase Conversion Rate
Definition:
- Journeys with Purchase stage
- Divided by journeys with Lead stage

Primary Use:
- Evaluate lead quality and dealer handoff effectiveness.

---

## Speed & Friction KPIs

### Time to Eligibility
Definition:
- Median time from first vehicle_view to submit_prequal

Primary Use:
- Detect UX or eligibility complexity issues.

### Time to Purchase
Definition:
- Median time from first vehicle_view to purchase_complete

Primary Use:
- Monitor journey compression and customer confidence.

---

## Personalization KPIs

### Recommendation Engagement Rate
Definition:
- Recommended vehicle clicks
- Divided by recommendation impressions

Primary Use:
- Measure relevance of personalized inventory.

### Incremental Lift (Experiment)
Definition:
- Difference in downstream conversion (Lead or Purchase)
- Between treatment and control groups

Primary Use:
- Decide whether personalization should be scaled, refined, or rolled back.

---

## Trigger Effectiveness KPIs

### Trigger Precision
Definition:
- Purchase or Lead rate among triggered users
- Compared to non-triggered eligible users

Primary Use:
- Prevent over-triggering and customer fatigue.
